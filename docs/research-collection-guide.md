# Research Collection Guide

Use this short process for each university:

1. Find the official university admissions page.
2. Record the main deadline and any department-specific deadline.
3. Find the department or laboratory page.
4. Add relevant professors to the master sheet.
5. Save links to ResearchGate and Google Scholar when available.
6. Record the citation count and the date you checked it.
7. Mark whether the professor is a good fit for your research interests.

## Recommended Fields

For every professor, try to collect:

- university name
- city
- department
- program
- professor name
- professor title
- research area
- lab name
- lab or profile URL
- ResearchGate URL
- Google Scholar URL
- citations
- checked date
- email
- application deadline
- status and notes

## Data Quality Tips

- Prefer official university or laboratory pages over third-party summaries.
- Keep the research area short and searchable.
- Add the exact date whenever you record citation counts.
- Use one row per professor to keep filtering easy in Excel.
